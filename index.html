About Clean Leaf International Corporation

Clean Leaf International Corporation, formerly known as Clean Leaf Industrial Sales, is a waste treatment and recovery company and a hazardoues and non-hazardous waste. transporter. It provides environment services through materials recovery and treatment of spent materials, market returns and condemned items. It is the only company in the Philippines with an "Environmental Technology Verification (ETV)" issued by the Department of Science and Technology (DOST) in coordination with the Department of Environment and Natural Resources (DENR). Services

Certified Treatment, Storage and Disposal

Clean Leaf International Corporation is a certified and trusted treatment, storage and disposal facility in compliance with the Republic Act 6969 which is known as the "Toxic Substances and Hazardous and Nuclear Wastes Control Act of 1990"

Certified Transporter

Clean Leaf International Corporation also offers container trucks which is called as "TRANSPORTER" in compliance with Republic Act 6969. The company is authorized to transport Hazardous and non-Hazardoues waste with their certified container trucks and driver.

Industrial Hazardous Waste Treatment

The facility is designed for the treatment of solid and liquid hazardous waste that contains large amount of organic pollutants. It utilizes physical - chemical treatment, an approach including phase seperation, such as through filtration, solidification into hard material allowing for landfill disposal and thermal hazardous waste treatment which is high temparature oxidation of wastes that converts them into gases and solid residues.


Wastewater Treatment

Wastewater treatment processes require careful management to ensure the protection of the water body that receives the discharge. We use physical, biological and chemical treatment to clean wastewater. This is accomplished by biological oxidation of organic compounds present in the system through microbial activity enhanced by well maintained aeration, pH, temperature. The seperation and settling of activated sludge solids is also incorporated in the process to create an acceptable quality of wastewater effluent.


Destruction for Non-Toxic Materials

Destruction of non-hazardous materials usually consists of expired consumer products and packaging. Such materials are destructed using crusher and other simple unit operations including size reduction and heat transfer with no chemical reactions. Treated materials are ready for disposal to landfill. Recyclable containers are manually destructed for recovery.
